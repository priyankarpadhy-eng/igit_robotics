01About Us

Building the future
of automation

IGIT Robotics is the technical flagship club of Indira Gandhi Institute of Technology, Sarang. Since 2015, we've united students passionate about robotics, automation, and intelligent systems.

From Arduino-based line followers to full autonomous SLAM-navigation platforms, our members tackle real engineering challenges guided by faculty mentors and industry advisors.

01
Hands-on Learning
Every member gets workshop access, hardware, and 1-on-1 mentorship from day one.
02
National Competitions
We represent IGIT at Robocon, TechFest IIT Bombay, e-Yantra, SIH, and more.
03
Research & Innovation
Student-led R&D with faculty collaboration, open-source contributions, and industry sponsors.
